Let's play Tic-Tac-Toe!

Tic Tac Toe is a game for two people who compete against each other in trying to place three signs next to each other in any direction in a grid.

To start playing, enter your names below and choose your signs. Click Start Game once the information is filled

When you're in the game, click on an empty square in the grid to place your sign. The player who manages to place three signs next to each other in any direction wins!

When the game is over, you can choose to play again, reset the score, or to go back to the main menu. Good luck and have fun!
